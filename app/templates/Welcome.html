ABOUT
70% of all projects lead to failure. That is a huge number. Our goal is to reduce that number with quayside.app, a project management web application designed to get teams started on projects quicker and answer the question “What’s next?”. quayside.app will uses a large language model(LLM) to break a project into deliverables with time estimations. quayside.app helps your team keep track of their tasks and remind them when to complete them. The idea of quayside was formulated in the summer of 2023 by Erik Williams, who has been a project manager for 7 years and is now the COO of Beadedstream. His vision for quayside is to “Ignite Collaborative Productivity”.
